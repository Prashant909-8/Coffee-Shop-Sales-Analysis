# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes coffee shop retail sales data using Microsoft Excel. The objective is to uncover meaningful business insights that can help improve sales performance, optimize operations, and support better business decision-making.

## Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data to gain actionable insights that enhance the performance and efficiency of a coffee shop.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Power Query
* Conditional Formatting
* Excel Formulas

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The analysis focuses on the following business metrics:

* Total Sales
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Order Value
* Average Items per Order
* Sales by Month
* Sales by Day of Week
* Sales by Hour
* Best-Selling Products

---

##  Key Insights

### 1. Sales by Day of Week

* The highest sales are recorded on **Monday**, **Thursday**, and **Friday**.
* These three days contribute significantly to overall weekly revenue, indicating higher customer traffic before and after the mid-week period.

### 2. Monthly Sales Trend

The sales contribution by month is:

| Month | Contribution |
| ----- | ------------ |
| April | 20%          |
| May   | 26%          |
| June  | 28%          |

**Insight:**

* Sales show a steady month-over-month increase.
* June generated the highest revenue, indicating positive business growth and increasing customer demand.

### 3. Peak Business Hours

* Peak sales occur between **8:00 AM and 10:00 AM**.
* The **highest sales are recorded at 10:00 AM**.

**Insight:**

* Morning hours are the busiest period.
* Staffing and inventory should be optimized during this time.

### 4. Customer Purchasing Behavior

* **Average Revenue per Order:** **$4.69**
* **Average Quantity per Order:** **1.44 items**

**Insight:**

* Customers typically purchase one to two items per visit.
* There is an opportunity to increase basket size through combo offers and upselling.

### 5. Best-Selling Products

The top-performing products are:

* Barista Espresso
* Brewed Black Tea
* Brewed Chai Tea
* Gourmet Brewed Coffee
* Hot Chocolate

**Insight:**
These beverages consistently generate the highest sales and should remain the primary focus for inventory management and promotional campaigns.

---

## Business Conclusion

The analysis reveals consistent business growth over the three-month period, with June achieving the highest sales contribution. Customer demand is strongest during weekday mornings, particularly between **8 AM and 10 AM**, making this the most critical operating window.

The best-selling beverages account for a substantial portion of total sales, while the average customer purchases only **1.44 items** per order, indicating room to increase average order size.

Overall, the coffee shop demonstrates healthy sales trends but has several opportunities to improve operational efficiency and revenue generation.

---

## Recommendations

* Increase staff availability during peak hours (8 AM–10 AM) to reduce waiting time and improve customer experience.
* Ensure sufficient inventory of the best-selling beverages to avoid stock shortages.
* Introduce combo meals or breakfast bundles to increase the average order quantity.
* Offer upselling opportunities, such as pastries or snacks with coffee purchases.
* Continue monitoring monthly sales trends to prepare inventory and staffing for periods of higher demand.
* Analyze underperforming products and consider revising their pricing, promotion, or menu placement.
